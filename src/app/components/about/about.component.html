The London Chess Club is dedicated to players of all ages and abilities. We hold regular blitz and rapid tournaments on-site, with occasional lectures and simul events (see schedule for upcoming events). Join our thriving chess community every Thursday evening at the Kiwanis Centre. Your first visit is free!
