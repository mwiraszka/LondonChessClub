Welcome to the LCC! The London Chess Club is open to players of all ages and abilities. We host regular blitz and rapid chess tournaments, as well as a variety of lectures, simuls and team competitions (see schedule for all upcoming events). Join our thriving chess community every Thursday evening at the Kiwanis Centre!
