The London Chess Club is open to players of all ages and abilities. We host regular blitz and rapid chess tournaments, as well as a variety of lectures, simuls and team competitions. Every fall we also host the London Chess Championship, a multi-section round-robin tournament to crown the city's next London Chess Champion.
