About the London Chess Club

Kiwanis Seniors' Community Centre

78 Riverside Drive

Thursdays, 6:00pm — 9:00pm

The London Chess Club is dedicated to players of all ages and abilities. We hold regular blitz and rapid tournaments on-site, with occasional lectures and simul events (see schedule for upcoming events). Come join our thriving chess community — first visit is free!

Have any questions or suggestions? Email us at londonchessclub­canada@gmail.com

Membership Fee

$40 per calendar year (Jan 1 — Dec 31), calculated pro rata (you only pay for the months remaining in the year).


Family discount: additional family members get 50% off.

Chess Sets and Clocks

Please bring your own chess sets and clocks. Don't own a set? No problem — you can purchase chess supplies at the club. See what we have available here.

Tournaments

Chess tournaments are held throughout the year. These include casual blitz tournaments, rapid tournaments, and the LCC Championship held in the fall. See schedule for a list of upcoming events.

Chess Ratings

We use our own London & Region rating system, managed by Gerry Litchfield. Current ratings can be found here.

Children

Children must be attended by a parent or guardian at all times.

Parking

Parking is free during chess club hours.
