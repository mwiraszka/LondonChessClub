Membership Fee

$40 per year (calculated pro rata), meaning you only pay for the remainder of the current calendar year (Jan 1st – Dec 31st).

Chess Supplies

Please bring your own chess sets and clocks. New to chess? No problem! We have supplies available for sale at the club. Contact Steve Demmery for more information.

Parking

There is plenty of parking space available behind the building, free of charge during chess club hours.

Chess Ratings

We use our own London & Region rating system, managed by Gerry Litchfield. Current ratings can be found here.

Rules of the Game

For tournaments we generally follow the laws of chess, set by the International Chess Federation (FIDE). Notable differences are the touch-move rule: we enforce touch-move during rated rapid tournaments but clock-move for our casual blitz tournaments.

Members are encouraged to try to resolve any disputes with their opponents before seeking arbitration. If you would like to speak to an arbiter, pause the clock and raise your hand to get their attention.

Tournaments

Chess tournaments are held throughout the year. These include casual blitz tournaments, rapid tournaments, and the London Chess Championship held in the fall. Our Blitz tournaments are unrated and free to play, with a time control of 10+0 (10 minutes for each player, with no increment after each move). Our Active tournaments are rated with a time control of 25+0. For our rated tournaments there is a nominal entry fee (usually $10) that goes towards the prize pool and covers any expenses in organizing the event.

See schedule for a list of upcoming events.

Every December we hold our Annual General Meeting (AGM) where we look back on the past year, review the club's finances, and elect any new board & committee members for the following year. All club members are invited to attend the AGM – see schedule for exact dates.

The board of directors meets monthly to go over the club's finances, plan out future events, and discuss any proposals brought forward by the club's members. You can read minutes from the meetings, as well as the LCC bylaws here.
