Membership Fee

$60 per year (calculated pro rata), meaning you only pay for the remainder of the current calendar year (Jan 1–Dec 31).

Chess Supplies

Please bring your own chess sets and clocks! New to chess? No problem — we have chess sets and scorebooks available for sale at the club.

Parking

There is plenty of parking space available behind the building, free of charge during chess club hours.

Chess Ratings

We use our own London & Region rating system, managed by Gerry Litchfield. Current ratings can be found here.

Rules of the Game

During tournament play we generally follow the laws of chess, set by the International Chess Federation (FIDE). Notable differences are the touch-move rule: we enforce touch-move during rated rapid tournaments but clock-move for our casual blitz tournaments.

Members are encouraged to try to resolve any disputes with their opponents before seeking arbitration. If still unresolved, pause the clock and raise your hand, and an arbiter will come help you out.

Tournaments

Chess tournaments are held throughout the year. These include casual blitz tournaments, rapid tournaments, and the London Chess Championship held in the fall. Our blitz tournaments are free to play, usually with a time-control of 10+0 (10 minutes for each player, with no increment after each move), whereas our rapid tournaments are usually 40+0. The rapid tournaments generally last 3–4 Thursdays to complete and are LCC rated. For longer tournaments there is a nominal entry fee that goes towards the prize pool and covers any expenses in organizing the event.
